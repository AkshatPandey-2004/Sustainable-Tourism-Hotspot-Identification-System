What is Sustainable Tourism?

Sustainable tourism is a type of tourism that takes into account the environmental, social, and economic impacts of travel and tourism. It aims to preserve and protect the natural and cultural heritage of destinations while providing economic benefits to local communities.

How it Works

Our system uses a variety of data sources and algorithms to identify and rank sustainable tourism destinations around the world. You can browse our rankings, learn more about each destination, and find ways to support sustainable tourism practices.
