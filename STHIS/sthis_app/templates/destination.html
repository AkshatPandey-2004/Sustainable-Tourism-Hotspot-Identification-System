Description

Nestled amidst the serene foothills of the Himalayas, Koti Village in Dehradun beckons travelers with its tranquil ambiance and breathtaking natural beauty. This charming village, enveloped by lush greenery and mist-clad mountains, offers a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. As you wander through its narrow lanes adorned with traditional wooden houses, you'll be greeted by warm smiles and genuine hospitality from the locals. Koti Village is a haven for nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers alike, offering enchanting trails for trekking, birdwatching, and nature walks.
